Workflow Management

Workflow Management - Tools for automating and tracking the progress of product data tasks and projects. This can include task assignments, deadlines, and status updates to help teams stay organized and on track.

User Management

Features that enable administrators to manage user accounts and access controls, such as user roles, permissions, and password policies. This can help ensure that only authorized users have access to product data.

Content Localization

Tools and processes for adapting product data for different languages and cultures. This can include translation tools, data modeling and taxonomy tools, and localization workflows to enable teams to create and manage localized content.

Integration

Technologies and processes for connecting the product master data management system with other systems and data sources, such as (enterprise resource planning) ERP, CRM, or supply chain management systems.

Product Catalog

Product catalog management - This can include tools for product data entry, data modeling and taxonomy, data cleansing and normalization, and data governance to ensure that product information is accurate and consistent.

Digital Asset

Tools for organizing, storing, and sharing digital assets such as images, videos, and documents related to product data. This can include file storage, file sharing, and file versioning to enable teams to work with digital assets more efficiently.

Personalization and Recommendation

Features that enable customers to shop and purchase products from their mobile devices, such as smartphones and tablets. This can include mobile-responsive design, mobile payment options, and integration with mobile marketing platforms to help teams reach and engage with their customers on mobile devices.

Order and inventory management

Tools for managing orders, inventory levels, and fulfillment processes more efficiently. This can include order tracking, inventory forecasting, and integration with fulfillment partners to help teams keep their products in stock and meet customer demand.
